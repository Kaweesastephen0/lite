About Lite Telecom

Connecting people through innovative technology since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Lite Telecom began as a small regional provider with a vision to revolutionize the telecom industry. Our founders, a team of engineers and customer service experts, were frustrated with the poor service and high prices offered by major carriers.

Starting with just 10 employees and a single office, we've grown to become one of the nation's most trusted telecom providers, serving over 2.5 million customers across all 50 states.

Our success is built on three core principles: innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We invest heavily in our network infrastructure while keeping our plans simple and affordable.
